VULNERABLE FROGS

This piece is a tribute to the most beatiful relationship that I have ever been involved in. I created this custom for a very special customer. It is made to represent the very essence of love itself inspired by the strongest connection to it that I have ever had.

"Vulnerable Frogs", Los Angeles CA

This piece was a special comission done for one of my customers. I was told to embody love in my own artistic style, and this was the result. Frogs may seem like an interesting approach to this, but for me the frog has always been a very respected spirit animal.

This is because the frog is a key element of many ancient traditions and spirtual healing rituals. In this way to bring two frogs together in love is to unite two powerful spirit guides together, representing a beatiful connection and bond of love.
