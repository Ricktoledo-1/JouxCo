STAY EXCITED

The spray can protruding from the flower within this piece is a representation of nature providing all of the tools necessary to create some of the most beautiful art. As nature is, in itself, the truest form of art.

"Stay Excited", Los Angeles CA, Date

The flower in this piece is providing the spray can to create new art with. This is a representation of nature's influence on my artwork. Nature has always provided me with most of my creative insight and much of my work is a direct reflection of this.

Nature is the source of all beauty and I feel that tapping into it, and drawing inspiration from it are some of the most important things that an artist can do to create their artwork.
